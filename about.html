Gamer Type & Favorite Games

My gamer type is KAES, Killer, Achiever, Explorer, and Socializer. I'm an 80% Killer, 60% Achiever, 47% Explorer, and 13% Socializer, and if I'm being completely honest, this is very accurate. I'm an introvert in real life and only babble to my closest of friends, so it makes sense for Socializer to be the lowest. For Explorer, it really depends on the game and what the worldbuilding is about. I like exploring, but after some time, it gets boring. Now onto the spicy stuff. Achiever, yes, yes, and yes. I am a very big Achiever, both in games and in real life. As I've said before, I'm the "goody-two-shoes" at school, so getting a 99 on anything is extremely painful because it's 1 point under perfection. In games, similar things happen. Take for example, the Google Doodle Championship Games. There are trophies in that game for helping out other characters, right? What I've done every time I've played that game is get all of the trophies, for both the minigames and for the interactions with the characters, and constantly check the trophy room with the suspicious bird to see if all of the trophies are there. The sense of satisfaction after seeing every single golden, glowering trophy in its spot is euphoric. Finally, the best one of them all: Killer. I'm honestly surprised that I'm only at 80%. I think the best example of me being in pure Killer mode is when I'm playing the Teams Mode in paper.io 2. Once I'm in a select team with my music playing in the background, I am in the zone. I don't care about increasing my team's dominance on the map, except for when I need to grow to induce a fight with a new enemy team. Seeing the kill count just going up after each bot kill is so purifying and satisfying that all of my anger just dissipates. Actually, very recently (From the date I'm writing this, 1/26/23), I reached my highest kill score yet, 54. I spent about 45 minutes on that one game and I was on a pure killing streak. Enemy teams were dying left and right as soon as I saw them eating away at my team in the small map in the corner of my screen. It's very therapeutic.

Moving on from the Gamer Types, here's some of my favorite games and why!

  1. Monopoly: I just like buying properties. I prefer the Here and Now Edition, it has an actual ending, which is when a player's passport fills up to the drawn line (Each property comes with a token). There's something about the trading, swapping, Chance cards, Here and Now cards, etc. that is very nostalgic to me. I also played this very often with my family and neighborhoood friends when I was younger, so there are a lot of memories connected to this game.
  2. Scrabble: I play this game for 2 reasons - 1) To try and beat my mother, who is extraordinarily good at this game, and 2) because my vocabulary is weaker than Mercury's gravity. So there's the drive of competition and academic improvement that stems from my playing of this game.
  3. Paper.io 2 (Teams Mode): This game is purely for me to rage and bring out my inner Killer Gamer Type. This is also the perfect way to put an evil smile on my face.
  4. Sweep: This game is a card game, one that has been played in my family for generations. The rules online are a little different from how my parents have taught me, but the essential root of the game is the same. I'm not going to explain the game here, but it's a game of skill, strategy, and the brain. I love strategy games like this and when my parents and I play, it's always a race to beat my father.
  5. Solitaire (Patience): Last but not least is Solitaire. This game I play just to see if I can win and to pass the time when I'm extremely bored and lonely. Just sitting on my bed with the cards set out in front of me and my music playing in the background is very relaxing. Unforntunately, this game is not very time-consuming, so I'm left bored once again after a few rounds.
